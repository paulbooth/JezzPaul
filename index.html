JEZZPAUL Help! (show/hide)

Left Click:Vertical line

Right Click (Mac: Two Finger Click): Horizontal line

Space Bar: change line direction

Swipe to create lines vertically or horizontally!

Don't hit a ball until the line is done growing or you lose! Once a line has stopped growing, the balls will bounce off and the blocked off region will be added to your score. Reduce the area of the balls to below a certain percentage to move onto the next level with more balls. SUPER FUN!
